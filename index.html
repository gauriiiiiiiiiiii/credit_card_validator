Luhn Algorithm Details

The Luhn algorithm is a simple checksum formula used to validate credit card numbers. It works by performing a series of calculations on the digits of the number and then checking if the final result is a multiple of 10.

  • Step 1: Double every second digit, starting from the rightmost digit.
  • Step 2: Sum the digits. For doubled digits > 9, add the two digits.
  • Step 3: Sum all digits from the original number (doubled and undoubled).
  • Step 4: If the total sum is a multiple of 10, the number is considered valid.
